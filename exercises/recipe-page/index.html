Gluten-Free Orange Chicken

Craving that crispy, tangy, sweet orange chicken from Panda Express but need it gluten-free? This recipe nails the flavor and crunch with a gluten-free twist, so you don't miss out on the takeout vibes. It's everything you love about the original, just made gluten-free at home.

Prep Time: 30 minutes Cook Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 1 hour Yields: 4 to 5

Ingredients

CHICKEN

  • 1 1/2 pounds chicken thighs, boneless and skinless
  • 1/2 cup gluten-free flour
  • 1 cup cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

ORANGE GLAZE

  • 1/4 cup coconut aminos
  • 1/3 cup orange juice
  • 1/3 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup water + 3 tablespoons cornstarch

OTHER

  • vegetable oil, for frying
  • green onions and sesame seeds, optional garnish

Instructions

MAKE THE ORANGE GLAZE

  1. Start by making the orange glaze. In a saucepan, combine the coconut aminos, orange juice, rice vinegar, brown sugar, sesame oil, garlic, ginger paste, and red pepper flakes. Bring it to a gentle boil over medium heat. Then reduce the heat to low.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the water and cornstarch until smooth. then slowly pour it into the bubbling sauce while stirring.

  3. Let it simmer for a few minutes until thick, glossy, and coats the back of a spoon. Turn off the heat and set it aside while you fry the chicken.
